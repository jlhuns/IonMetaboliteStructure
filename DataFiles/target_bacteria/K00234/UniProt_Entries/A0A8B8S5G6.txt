CC   -!- FUNCTION: Flavoprotein (FP) subunit of succinate dehydrogenase (SDH)
CC       that is involved in complex II of the mitochondrial electron transport
CC       chain and is responsible for transferring electrons from succinate to
CC       ubiquinone (coenzyme Q). SDH also oxidizes malate to the non-canonical
CC       enol form of oxaloacetate, enol-oxaloacetate. Enol-oxaloacetate, which
CC       is a potent inhibitor of the succinate dehydrogenase activity, is
CC       further isomerized into keto-oxaloacetate. Can act as a tumor
CC       suppressor. {ECO:0000256|ARBA:ARBA00045977}.
