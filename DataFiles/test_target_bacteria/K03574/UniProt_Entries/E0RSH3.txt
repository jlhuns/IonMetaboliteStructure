CC   -!- FUNCTION: Oxidized purine nucleoside triphosphate hydrolase which is a
CC       prominent sanitizer of the oxidized nucleotide pool. Catalyzes the
CC       hydrolysis of 2-oxo-dATP (2-hydroxy-dATP) into 2-oxo-dAMP. Has also a
CC       significant hydrolase activity toward 2-oxo-ATP, 8-oxo-dGTP and 8-oxo-
CC       dATP. Through the hydrolysis of oxidized purine nucleoside
CC       triphosphates, prevents their incorporation into DNA and the subsequent
CC       transversions A:T to C:G and G:C to T:A. Also catalyzes the hydrolysis
CC       of methylated purine nucleoside triphosphate preventing their
CC       integration into DNA. Through this antimutagenic activity protects
CC       cells from oxidative stress. {ECO:0000256|ARBA:ARBA00045200}.
