CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the attachment of isoleucine to tRNA(Ile). As IleRS

CC       activated Val-AMP. The other activity is designated 'posttransfer'
CC       editing and involves deacylation of mischarged Val-tRNA(Ile).
