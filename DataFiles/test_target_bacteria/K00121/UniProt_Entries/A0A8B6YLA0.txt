CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the oxidation of long-chain primary alcohols and
CC       the oxidation of S-(hydroxymethyl) glutathione. Also oxidizes long
CC       chain omega-hydroxy fatty acids, such as 20-HETE, producing both the
CC       intermediate aldehyde, 20-oxoarachidonate and the end product, a
CC       dicarboxylic acid, (5Z,8Z,11Z,14Z)-eicosatetraenedioate. Class-III ADH
CC       is remarkably ineffective in oxidizing ethanol. Required for clearance
CC       of cellular formaldehyde, a cytotoxic and carcinogenic metabolite that
CC       induces DNA damage. Also acts as a S-nitroso-glutathione reductase by
CC       catalyzing the NADH-dependent reduction of S-nitrosoglutathione,
CC       thereby regulating protein S-nitrosylation.
CC       {ECO:0000256|ARBA:ARBA00045297}.
