CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the pyruvate dehydrogenase complex (PDHc) that is a
CC       multi-enzyme complex composed of multiple copies of three enzymes,
CC       pyruvate dehydrogenase (subunits PDH1A and PDHB, E1 component),
CC       dihydrolipoamide acetyltransferase (DLAT, E2 component), and
CC       dihydrolipoamide dehydrogenase (DLD, E3 component) to which is added an
CC       additional protein the E3-binding protein (PDHX, E3BP). In terms of
CC       structural architecture, the E2 and E3BP components assemble into a
CC       60meric central core with icosahedral symmetry. The central core is
CC       decorated with E1 and E3 proteins. Currently, two alternative models
CC       for the E2:E3BP stoichiometry are considered as being either 48:12
CC       (E2(48)-E3BP(12)) or 40:20 (E2(40)-E3BP(20)). Interacts with PDK2 and
CC       PDK3. Interacts with SIRT4. Interacts with PDHB.
